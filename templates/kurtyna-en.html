Art in Architecture

Discover Curtain House

Where Art Meets Nature

Curtain House, a creation of Trzop Architects, is a testament to how modern materials and creative thinking can transform living space into a work of art. Designed with art enthusiasts in mind, it combines the rawness of concrete with the warmth of natural light and surrounding greenery. It represents a harmonious blend of modern design with the comfort of home, while offering spectacular views of the Mazovian nature.

Trivia about Curtain House

Discover the Unknown

Concrete Elegance

Curtain House utilizes concrete not only as a structural material but also as an artistic one, creating facades resembling gently waving curtains.

Glass and Light

Glazed walls open the interiors to the external landscape, providing continuity of space and extraordinary visual experiences.

Architectural Symphony

Two mutually perpendicular volumes of the building create a unique spatial composition that harmoniously interacts with the natural surroundings.

Curtain at EliteHome

Where Architecture Meets Art

As the general contractor, EliteHome takes pride in the realization of Curtain House, a project by Trzop Architects. This extraordinary residence, located in the heart of Mazovia, embodies the essence of modern design, combining the rawness of concrete with the warmth of a home fireplace and openness to nature. Innovative approach to materials and meticulous attention to detail make this house unique in our portfolio, demonstrating our commitment to creating spaces that inspire and transcend the traditional concept of home.
