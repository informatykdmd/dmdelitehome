Discover Forest Core

A Modern Residence Immersed in Nature

Forest Core is a luxurious residence located in Izabelin, designed by MAG Architekci. Nestled deep in the forest, the building blends contemporary minimalism with the serenity of nature. Its L-shaped layout, expansive glazing, and use of natural materials like wood and stone make the house harmonize beautifully with its surroundings. It offers privacy, light, and balance — perfect for those who value elegance, peace, and a deep connection with nature.

A Luxurious Residence Surrounded by Nature

Forest Core, designed by MAG Architekci, is a remarkable example of modern architecture seamlessly integrated into a natural setting. Located deep in the forest of Izabelin, this residence offers complete privacy and closeness to nature without compromising on premium finishes or cutting-edge technology. Its L-shaped form, vast glazing, and use of wood and stone make the house part of the landscape itself. It’s a space crafted for those who seek harmony, elegance, and tranquillity.
