Circle Wood

2018 - 2020

The Art of Nature
Architecture in Harmony

In the Embrace of the Forest

Elegance and Nature

Circle Wood House, designed by Przemek Olczyk of Mobius Architects, is the culmination of the search for harmony between modern architecture and wild nature. Located in the heart of Kampinos National Park, this house draws inspiration from the form and structure of trees, creating a space where the boundaries between the interior and the environment blur. Awarded at the European Property Awards, Circle Wood House is a testament that architecture can simultaneously be art and a refuge.

Circle Wood House

Fun Facts

European Property Awards

Circle Wood House has been honored in the prestigious competition, emphasizing its innovation and beauty in the "Architecture Single Residence" category.

Indoor Trees

The unique atrium with tall pine trees in the central part of the house brings a forest landscape element indoors, creating a lively artistic space.

Concrete and Okoume Wood

The concrete structure of the house has been clad with panels made of exotic Okoume wood, not only blending the building into its forest surroundings but also giving it a unique elegance.

Circle Wood in EliteHome

Harmony and Art

As proud executors of the Circle Wood House, EliteHome presents this exceptional realization by Przemek Olczyk from Mobius Architects. Nestled within the landscape of Izabelin, the house blends modernity with nature, creating a space that serves as both an intimate retreat and a work of art. Through the application of innovative solutions and materials, Circle Wood House stands as a flagship example of our commitment to creating homes that transcend the boundaries of traditional architecture and thrive in symbiosis with their surrounding environment.
