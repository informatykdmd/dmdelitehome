Discover Dune House

Harmony of Space

Dune House, a creation of Mobius Architects, combines innovative architecture with the picturesque landscape of the Narew River. Its unique form, resembling a dune, integrates with the surroundings, offering unparalleled views and privacy. This architectural gem near Warsaw is an example of perfect symbiosis between a building and nature.

Secrets of Dune House

Discover the Unknown

Harmonious Integration

Dune House perfectly blends into the landscape, resembling a natural dune, making it invisible from the road and emphasizing its private nature.

Architectural Harp

Its arc-like form, akin to a harp, vibrates with the landscape, creating a unique connection with nature and opening the interior to the vast views of the Narew River.

Innovation and Comfort

The house is equipped with a range of modern solutions, including two swimming pools, helipads, and automated elements, enhancing living comfort.

Dune House Stands Out

Dune at EliteHome

A Jewel of Architecture

Dune House, designed by Przemek Olczyk from Mobius Architects, is the quintessence of luxury and modernity. Located in a picturesque landscape near Warsaw, it combines advanced architecture with the natural beauty of its surroundings. The realization of this project is proof of how innovative design approaches can create spaces that are both functional and inspiring. EliteHome, serving as the general contractor, is immensely proud of this flagship project, which not only raises the standards of contemporary construction but also sets new trends in harmoniously integrating living spaces with the surrounding nature.
