Discover Wind House

Unique Architecture

Wind House, designed by Przemek Olczyk from Mobius Architects, is the epitome of the symbiosis of modern architecture with the wild nature of Izabelin, an extension of the Kampinos Forest. The house, with its unique structure, sprawls amidst a pine forest, creating a space where the boundaries between interior and exterior seem to blur. This extraordinary villa, covering an area of 450 m², is a true work of art, created with the idea of harmonious coexistence with nature.

Wind House

House Trivia

Nature-Inspired Design

Przemek Olczyk created a house whose form was dictated by the wooded areas of the plot, resulting in a unique shape of the structure.

Open Structure

The structure of the house intentionally remains open, integrating the interior with the dense forest and allowing direct contact with nature.

Material Harmony

Facades made of Siberian larch and elements of natural stone blend perfectly into the forest environment, blurring the boundaries between the building and nature.

Icon of Modernity

EliteHome proudly presents Wind House, a project that perfectly combines innovative design with respect for the surrounding nature. Designed by Przemek Olczyk from Mobius Architects, the house offers a unique experience of living among trees, with a focus on continuous dialogue with nature. With its open spaces, thoughtful integration with the forest, and use of natural materials, Wind House is a testament to our commitment to creating architecture that harmonizes with the landscape and sets new standards in design.
