Privacy Policy

Preliminary Information
We care about your privacy and want you to be informed about the way we collect, use, and protect your personal data. Please read the following information regarding our Privacy Policy:
Collection and Processing of Personal Data
We make every effort to protect your personal data. We only collect the necessary information, such as your name and email address, to provide you with relevant updates and information about our community.

Your personal data is processed in order to provide information, offers, and to personalize the experience associated with our community.
Data Security
We ensure that your data is safe with us. We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized access or loss.
Sharing Data
We do not share your personal data with third parties without your express consent. We care about our users' privacy.
User Rights
You have the right to access, correct, delete, or limit the processing of your personal data. Contact us if you wish to exercise these rights.
Transferring Data to Other Countries
In some cases, your personal data may be transferred to other countries in accordance with applicable law.
Cookies and Tracking Technologies
We use cookies and other tracking technologies to analyze site traffic, personalize content, and deliver advertisements. You can manage your cookie settings in your browser.
Data Transmission Security
We have taken security measures to protect data transmission, however, we cannot guarantee absolute security when transmitting data over the internet.
Contact Us
If you have any questions regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us at biuro@dmdelitehome.pl.
Consent to Data Processing
By using our community, you consent to the processing of your personal data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
Notification of Changes
Information about changes to the Privacy Policy will be provided on our website.
Applicability to Children
Our community is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under this age.
Direct Marketing
We may send you marketing information only after obtaining your express consent. You can unsubscribe at any time.
Changing or Deleting Data
You have the right to request a change or deletion of your personal data. Contact us to implement these requests.
Important Legal Information
