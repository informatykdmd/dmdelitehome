Discover Lesznowolska House

Unconventional Shape in Urban Setting

Lesznowolska House, a project by Jio - Jankowski and Opyrchał studio, is a unique combination of modern architecture with openness to nature. Situated in an urban environment, surrounded by greenery, the building stands out with irregular, "random" glazing that allows abundant light into the interior. Its interiors, characterized by a light, informal boho style, create a space full of relaxation and freedom, ideal for those who appreciate originality and closeness to nature.

Lesznowolska

Trivia

Irregular Glazing

Unusual, irregular glazing not only gives the building a unique character but also maximizes the experience of connection with the surrounding garden and lets natural light into the house.

Boho Style

The interiors of the house, styled in boho fashion, are characterized by casual elegance, the use of natural materials and textures, creating a cozy and inspiring living space.

Harmony with Nature

The Lesznowolska house project, with its openness to the external garden and urban environment, shows how modern architecture can harmoniously coexist with nature, even in the city.

Lesznowolska House at EliteHome

Urban Oasis in Boho Style

The realization of Lesznowolska House by Jio - Jankowski and Opyrchał studio sets an example of integrating modern architecture with the natural environment. Located in the heart of the city yet open to a private garden, the house is a space where modernity meets nature. It's designed for those who wish to live in style while appreciating closeness to nature. With the use of open spaces, irregular glazing, and boho style, Lesznowolska exemplifies a home that is both modern and incredibly cozy.
