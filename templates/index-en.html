Dune House

Luxury Villa by the Narew River

Curved like a seaside dune, the Dune House is an impressive project by Mobius Architects, which integrated with the picturesque hill by the Narew River.

  1. Dune House
    2019 - 2021

    Spanning 1500 m2, a minimalist structure of white plaster and stone extends, hiding within its interior bedrooms, a living room with a kitchen, a recreational area, and even two swimming pools and helipads.

  2. Curtain House
    2015 - 2017

    A simple shape with a distinctive "curtain" of concrete facade conceals spacious interiors filled with natural light. Large glass windows facing the garden blur the boundaries between the interior and the exterior, creating an intimate space for relaxation.

  3. Circle Wood
    2018 - 2020

    Inspired by a cut tree trunk, Circle Wood House offers its residents a sense of privacy and intimate contact with nature.
