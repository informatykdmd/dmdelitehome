FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQ section!
Below you will find answers to the most frequently asked questions about our platform. If you have additional questions, please contact us.
How can I register on the website?
To register, simply sign up for our Newsletter using the form in the footer. Provide the required information and follow the instructions.
How can I change my name in the subscription?
To change your name in the subscription, write from your registered email address to biuro@dmdelitehome.pl with your request, and we will send you feedback.
How can I update my address in the subscription?
To update your address in the subscription, re-register for our Newsletter using the form in the footer.
Is it possible to cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Send a message from your registered email address to biuro@dmdelitehome.pl with your request, and we will send you feedback. Remember, after cancelling your subscription, you will lose access to commenting and conversations on the platform.
Can I use the platform on more than one device?
Yes, our platform allows usage across multiple devices.
How can I contact the technical support team?
If you have questions or encounter problems, use the contact form available in the Help section or write directly to the email address of our support department: biuro@dmdelitehome.pl.
