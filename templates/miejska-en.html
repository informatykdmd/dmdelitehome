Discover the Pearl of Bielany

Architectural Harmony

The Urban Villa in Stare Bielany, designed by MAG Architects under the direction of Marcin Grzyb, sets an example of perfect integration of modern architecture with the historical character of the district. Stretching over 450 m², the residence combines elegance and functionality, making it an object of admiration and inspiration for residents. On one side, an imposing facade facing the street, on the other - a picturesque garden facing the courtyard, creating a space full of peace and beauty in the heart of Warsaw.

Urban Villa: Bielany Pride

Modernism in Harmony with the City

The Urban Villa in Stare Bielany, a masterpiece by Marcin Grzyb from MAG Architects, is the epitome of blending modern design with the traditional spirit of Warsaw's Bielany district. This unique project not only enriches the architectural landscape of the neighborhood but also provides a space where luxury meets everyday functionality. Offering residents 450 m² of thoughtfully designed space, along with a charming garden, the Urban Villa reflects the pursuit of beauty and tranquility in a dynamic urban environment.
